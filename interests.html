What am I interested in besides breathing?


I code.

Although at first it made me rage-quit, I feel like I am progressively falling in love with coding, because it gives me a new field of understanding and the possibility to do anything that can contribute to help this little world of ours.

The Lick.

Music is my passion, music is what keeps me alive. I'm a somewhat decent guitar player, pianist and composer, although I have wasted so much potential hours of practice that could have brought me to be more profficient, virtuoso and would have helped me express my feelings to the world in a better way.
