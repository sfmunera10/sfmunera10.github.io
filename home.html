Well...I guess I'll introduce myself:

Santiago Múnera

Systems and computing engineer undergraduate student at Universidad De Los Andes


"I would describe myself as an ambivalent person, looking for knowledge, wisdom and thrills in order to nurture myself and kindly help others along my way too."

Hello there! First of all, I want to express my sincere thanks to you, my spectator, for having a look at this site. It means so much for me that you to take little of your time to check out a brief description of myself, what some of my interests are, some cool things that I've done and my overall capabilities.

So, without further ado, you get to know my full name as Santiago Felipe Múnera Dávila, a guy born and raised in his early years in the city of Cúcuta, Norte De Santander, a beautiful town located in the northeast of Colombia, near the border with Venezuela. At the age of 6, I had to move with my family (My parents and my sister) to the city of Bucaramanga (still in Colombia but located in the state of Santander) and live there for a year, due to the fact that my father works in Bogotá (Colombia's capital city), we needed to be as close as possible to the places where my father was sent to work but at the same time keep in touch with the family that I had at Cúcuta. Finally, after spending that year in Bucaramanga, we moved to Bogotá to finally settle our lives in this capital.

Currently, I'm 22 years old and I'm still living in Bogotá with my parents and sister. Moreover, if I take into consideration the things that I do, I would label myself as a systems and computing engineer student, a mostly empirical musician, kind of a calisthenics and fitness aficionado, an artist and a programmer who wants to get the most of out his development skills. Lastly, I would also describe myself as an ambivalent person, looking for knowledge, wisdom and thrills in order to nurture myself and kindly help others along my way too.
